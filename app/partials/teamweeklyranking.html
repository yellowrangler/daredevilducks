Team Weekly Ranking

Click me to toggle Information for Weekly Ranking

This Information displays the Ranking for the team selected for each week that has been played and the ranking of the Team the selected team played against. The idea is to give you a idea of how the seleted team is progressing and how they may play against similar teams.

The last entry will be for the current week which be a regurgitation of the previous weeks stats for each team. The purpose being to show you the rankings for the teams that are playing for the current week!

Week The week that these statistics are created from.
Team Name The Name of the Teams which played. The Team in red is the team you seleted.
Score The Score of that particular Game. The score in green is the winnig score.
W The Wins for that Team at the time the game was played.
L The Losses for that Team at the time the game was played.
T The Ties for that Team at the time the game was played.
PR The Power Ranking for the team at the time the game was played.
T The Total Offensive Ranking for the team at the time the game was played.
S The Offensive Scoring Ranking for the team at the time the game was played.
P The Offensive Passing Ranking for the team at the time the game was played.
R The Offensive Rushing Ranking for the team at the time the game was played.
T The Total Defensive Ranking for the team at the time the game was played.
S The Defensive Scoring Ranking for the team at the time the game was played.
P The Defensive Passing Ranking for the team at the time the game was played.
R The Defensive Rushing Ranking for the team at the time the game was played.

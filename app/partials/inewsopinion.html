Conference Review! Prepare for the Super Bowl!

What an amazing Conference playoff week!

In the AFC playoffs the Patriots beat the Bo Nix-less Broncos. Teams were tied coming out for the 2nd half. The snow was already creating large problems on the playing field. A miscue by the Broncos Stidham gave the Patriots a field goal which ended up being the difference!

In the NFC playoffs the Seahawks beat the Rams. This was an exciting high scoring game. No snow for this game! Only beautiful California weather. Both teams were able to move the ball up and down the field for 3 quarters. But in the end the Seahawks just made too many plays.

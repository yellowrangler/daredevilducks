Divisional Playoffs in Review! Prepare for Conference finals!

What an amazing weekend of playoffs! I can't remember how many times I said - Did I just see that? This playoff week had it all - snow storms, freezing temperatures, crazy passes, crushing last second defeats - Yowsa!

The Broncos beat the Bills in a game that was a crushing thriller with an O'Henry plotline. The game went back and forth! Both teams played with great heart! What looked like a last minute touchdown throw by the Bills for the win, morphed into an amazing interception by the Broncos. But wait, there is more! At the post game conference, the Broncos coach Sean Payton announced that their starting quarterback Bo Nix had broken a bone in his foot and would be out for the rest of the season!

The Seahawks crushed, humiliated, embarrassed the 49ers in a game where the poor 49ers could do no right!

The Patriots beat the favored Texans in a turnover extravaganza by both teams! This was a contest dominated by both defenses in a snow storm. In the end the Patriots were able to convert turnovers to points where the Texans could not.

The Rams beat the Bears in Chicago! The Bears tied the game up on a Hail Mary last second pass to put the game into overtime! In overtime the Rams intercepted a pass by the Bears and turned it into a field goal win!

All in all a superb week of football!

Week 8 recap!

In the first game the Bills beat a competitive Buccaneers team. The Panthers eked out their first win over the Texans. The Saints beat the Colts in Indianapolis. The Cowboys beat the Rams. The Vikings beat the disappointing Packers. The Dolphins beat the Patriots.

In the battle for New York, the Jets beat their state rival Giants. The Titans behind Levis beat the Falcons. The Jaguars beat the Steelers. In a slug fest, the Eagles beat the Commanders. The Seahawks beat the Browns. The Ravens beat the Cardinals. In the upset of the week, the Broncos crushed the Chiefs. The Chargers beat the Bears.

In the Monday night game, the Lions beat the Raiders!

All in all a superb week of football! On to week 9!

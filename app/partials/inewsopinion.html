End of week 13 for the 2021 Season!

The week got started with the Cowboys beating the Saints.

The Dolphins crushed the Giants. The Colts whopped on the Texans. The Lions took their first complete win from the Vikings. The Eagles beat the Jets. The Cardinals beat the Bears. The Chargers beat the Bengals. The Buccaneers beat the Falcons, to stop their loosing skid. The Rams crushed the Jaguars. The Redskins beat the Raiders. The Steelers beat the Ravens.

The Seahawks beat the 49ers. The Chiefs beat the Broncos.

In the last game of the week, the Patriots beat the Bills in Buffalo!

All in all a great week of football!

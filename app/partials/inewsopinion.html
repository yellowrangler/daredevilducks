End of Conference Playoffs. Prepare for the Super Bowl!

On Sunday, the Bengals stunned the football pedigree by beating the heavily favored Kansas City Chiefs. It was a game of two halves with the Chiefs taking a 18 point lead in the first half. In the second half the Bengals came back and tied the game, sending it into overtime. The Chiefs won the coin toss but Mahomes was intercepted. The Bengals never looked back, getting close enough to make the game winning field goal; catapulting them into the Super Bowl!

In the second game, the Rams beat the 49ers in a close contest. The Rams took the final lead late in the 4th quarter leaving the the 49ers under 2 minutes on the clock. Garoppolo was unable to move the team. In fact he threw an interception that confirmed that the Rams would be in the Super Bowl!

All in all a great week of Conference football!

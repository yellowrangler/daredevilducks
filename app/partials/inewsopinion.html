Divisional Playoffs Week 20!

In the Saturday Wildcard games - the Texans beat the Chargers. In the night game the Ravens crushed the Steelers!

In Sunday Wildcard games - The Bills stampeded over the Broncos. In spite of the large margin of loss, the Broncos look primed for next year. The Eagles easily dispatched the Packers. The Commanders beat the Buccaneers in an exciting contest.

In the Monday night Wildcard game - The Rams trampled the Vikings. This was a disappointing loss for a Vikings team that showed so much prowess during the regular season.

All in all a superb week of playoff football!

Week 16 in Review! Prepare for Week 17!

The Curtain is beginning to come down on the regular 2025 season. Five of the seven playoff spots are now known. So we have only two spots up for grabs! On my radar is the last game of the regular season. If the Buccaneers are able to win this weeks game against the Dolphins in Miami and if the Panthers loose to the Seahawks in Seattle, then the last game will decide who takes the NFC South division! Stay tuned!

On the Thursday game the Seahawks beat the Rams in a nail biter!

In Saturday's games the Eagles beat the Commanders. The Bears won over the Packers in one of the most trilling games of the week!

In Sunday's games the The Panthers beat the Buccaneers. The Bills beat the Browns. The Chargers knocked the Cowboys out of the playoffs in Dallas! The Saints crushed the Jets! The Titans beat the Chiefs. The Bengals abused the Dolphins in Miami! The Falcons beat the Cardinals. In an upset of sorts, the Jaguars beat the Broncos. The Steelers beat the Lions in Detroit! The Texans beat the Raiders but then who doesn't!

In the Monday night game the 49ers beat Colts!

All in all a superb week of football!

Week 1 is in the Books! Prepare for Week 2!

What a great start to the season! I can't remember a season start with so many not only close games, but unbelievable comebacks!

In the Thursday night game the Eagles retained their Super Bowl swagger beating the Cowboys.

In a special Friday night game the Chargers beat the heavily favored Chiefs in Brazil!

In Sunday's games the Buccaneers beat the Falcons in a thriller. The Bengals beat the Browns in another thrilling and close contest! The Colts came out firing on all cylinders as they squished the Dolphins. The Jaguars crushed the hapless Panthers.

The Raiders beat the Patriots in New England. The Cardinals beat the Saints in a another thriller! The Steelers with their new QB Arron Rodgers, beat the Jets in New York. The Commanders crushed the Giants.

The Broncos beat the Titans and 49ers beat the Seahawks in Seattle. The Packers crushed the Lions. The Rams beat the Texans.

In the Sunday night game the Bills beat the Ravens in again another amazing thriller and comeback!

In the Monday night game the Vikings beat the Bears in Chicago in yet again another amazing thriller and comeback!

All in all a superb week of football!

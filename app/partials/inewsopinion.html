Week 9 recap!

In the first game the Steelers beat the Titans. The Chiefs beat the Dolphins in Germany. In one of the more entertaining contests, the Vikings came back and beat the Falcons. The Browns beat the Cardinals. The Packers beat the Rams. In an amazing back and forth affair, the Texans showed their mettle as they came back to beat the Buccaneers.

The Saints beat the Bears. The Commanders beat the disappearing Patriots. In the war of the birds, the Ravens beat the Seahawks evermore! The Colts beat the Panthers. The Eagles beat their division rival Cowboys. The Raiders beat the Giants. The Bengals beat the Bills.

In the Monday night game, the Chargers crushed the Jets!

All in all a superb week of football! On to week 10!

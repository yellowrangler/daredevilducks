End of week 4 for the 2021 Season!

This week got started with the Bengals beating the Jaguars in a close contest.

The Jets go their first win in overtime against the Titans. The Chiefs beat the Eagles and the Cowboys gave th ePanthers their firt loss. The Giants got their first win of the season over the Saints in New Orleans! In a low scorring affair, the Browns beat the Vikings. The Bears got their second win over the still winless Lions. All in all a great week of football!

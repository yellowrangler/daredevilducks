Week 14 is in the Books! Prepare for Week 15!

The were a number of big games this week that served as harbingers for things to come! The most compelling to me was the Texans winning out over the Chiefs in Kansas. In last years playoffs the Chiefs beat the Texans in a game with a number of controversial critical referee calls in favor of the Chiefs. The Chiefs ended up winning that game. Now, with the Chiefs on the ropes regarding the playoffs, the Texans got a sense of retribution as they beat them at home. Can't wait to see what happens next week!

On the Thursday game the Lions beat the Cowboys in a competitive match.

In Sunday's games the Seahawks crushed the Falcons in Atlanta. The Titans eked out a win over the Browns in Cleveland! The Jaguars beat the Colts in a game where the Colts starting quarterback, Daniel Jones, was injured and is out for the season. The Vikings shutout the Commanders. The Dolphins beat the Jets in New York! The Steelers beat the Ravens in Baltimore!

In an upset, the Saints beat the Buccaneers in Tampa. The Bills came from behind to beat the Bengals! The Broncos beat the Raiders in Las Vegas. The Packers held on to beat the Bears! The Rams crushed the Cardinals in Phoenix!

In the Sunday night game the Texans had a statement win against the Chiefs in Kansas!

In the Monday night game the Chargers beat the Eagles in overtime!

All in all a superb week of football!

End of the Wild Card Weekend. Prepare for Divisional Playoffs!

On Saturday, the Bengals beat the Raider in a close contest. The Bills crushed and embarrassed the Patriots.

On Sunday, the Buccaneers beat the Eagles. The 49ers beat the Cowboys in a game where the Cowboys basically shot themselves in the head all game long with penalties. The Steelers miracle season ended with a loss to the Chiefs.

All in all a great Wild Card week of football!

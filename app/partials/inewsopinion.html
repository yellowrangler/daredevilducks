Week 11 is in the Books! Prepare for Week 12!

What a week! The big game of the week was the Broncos vs the Chiefs. A great defensive contest that went down to a last second field goal by the Broncos! The Patriots continue to roll with a win at home against the Jets! Can't wait to see what happens next week!

In the Thursday night game the Patriots beat the Jets.

In Sunday's games the Dolphins defeated the Commanders. The Panthers beat the Falcons in Atlanta! The Bills got back into their winning ways as they dispatched a very good Buccaneers team. The Jaguars beat the mercurial Chargers!

The Bears clawed the Vikings. The Packers held on to beat the Giants in New York! The Texans lassoed the Titans. The Steelers pummeled the Bengals.

The 49ers dispatched the Cardinals. The Ravens beat the Browns in Cleveland! The Broncos were last man standing as they beat the Chiefs!

In the Sunday night game the Eagles beat the Lions.

In the Monday night game the Cowboys beat the Raiders in Las Vegas!

All in all a superb week of football!

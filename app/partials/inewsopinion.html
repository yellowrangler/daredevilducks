Week 18 in Review! Prepare for Wild Card Weekend!

The regular season is over! With apologies to Glen Frey, the heat is on! It's time for the cream to rise to the top! I love this time of the year! The playoff dance card is filled! All that's left is have at it! Play Ball!

In Saturday's games the Buccaneers beat the Panthers. The Seahawks dispatched the 49ers!

In Sunday's games the Falcons beat the Saints. The Browns beat the Bengals in Cincinnati. The Texans beat the Colts! The Jaguars crushed the Titans! The Vikings beat the Packers.

The Giants mauled the Cowboys! The Bills stampeded the Jets. The Lions beat the Bears! The Broncos crushed the Chargers! The Patriots humbled Dolphins!

The Commanders mauled the Eagles in Philadelphia! The Raiders beat the Chiefs. The Rams dismissed the Cardinals! In one of the more exciting nail bitters the Steelers beat the Ravens to take take the AFC Northern Division!

All in all a superb week of football!

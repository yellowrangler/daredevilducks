End of week 14 for the 2021 Season!

The week got started with the Vikings beating the Steelers.

The Cowboys held on to beat the Redskins. The Titans shut out the Jaguars. The Seahawks stayed viable by dispatching the Texans. The Chiefs crushed the Raiders. The Saints embarrassed the Jets in New York. The Falcons eeked by the Panthers.

The Browns beat the Ravens. The Chargers beat the woeful Giants. The Broncos crushed the cowardly Lions. The 49ers held on to beat the mercurial Bengals. The Bills lost in a heart breaker to the Tom Brady Buccaneers. The Packers beat the Bears.

In the last game of the week, the Rams upset the Cardinals in Arizona!

All in all a great week of football!

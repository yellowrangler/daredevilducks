Week 13 is in the Books! Prepare for Week 14!

The big game this week was the Chiefs against the Cowboys in Dallas. Both teams needed the win and a loss for the the Chiefs would put a serious dent in their usual swagger! Well - swagger be gone as the Cowboys made the Dallas crowd proud! Can't wait to see what happens next week!

On the Thursday Turkey day games the Packers beat the Lions. The Cowboys beat the favored Chiefs in an upset! In another upset the Bengals beat the highly favored Ravens in Baltimore!

In the Friday night game the Bears beat the Eagles in Philadelphia!

In Sunday's games the underdog Panthers defeated the highly favored Rams. The 49ers dispatched the Browns in Cleveland! The Texans beat the favored Colts in Indiana. The Dolphins beat the Saints.

The Jets beat Falcons! The Jaguars beat the Titans in Tennessee! The Buccaneers got back to winning as they beat the Cardinals! The Seahawks crushed the Vikings.

The Bills stampeded the Steelers. The Chargers electrocuted the Raiders!

In the Sunday night game the Broncos eked out a win against the Commanders in Washington!

In the Monday night game the Patriots retained best record as they beat the Giants!

All in all a superb week of football!

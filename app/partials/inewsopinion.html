End of Conference Playoffs. Prepare for Divisional weekend!

On Saturday, the Bengals beat the Titans in a close contest that was won by a last second field goal. The 49ers beat the number 1 seeded Packers, and again by a last second field goal.

On Sunday, the Rams beat the Buccaneers, again with a last second field goal! The Chiefs beat the Bills in a game that went into overtime, with the Chiefs winning the coin toss and ultimately the game.

All in all a great Divisional week of football!

Total Wins Leaders Help

Summary

The Total Wins Leaders page is where a member can view member rankings based on game wins. This is were bragging rights start!

You can select the Season, Game Type and Member Groups. You can also Show or Hide the addition of Experts to the ranking list.

The Experts are football professionals that pick games during the season. We collect their picks and add them so that you can see how you and others compare.

The Member Groups are requested name groups of members. If you wish to create a group you must reach out and make a request to us. No Charge! Groups can be last names, club names, school chums anything you want. Selecting a Member Groups will reset the list to show rankings within that group. You may be fifteenth against full member ranking; But you can be number one in your group! It's all relative.

The ranking list displays all members in wins ranking order

Details
Selecting and viewing the results

This is where you can select Season, Game Type, Member Groups and Experts.

The Season defaults to the current season. Changing the season will bring up rankings associated with that year.

The Game Type defaults to the regular season. You can choose Total Season, Regular Season or Post Season.

Regular Season: This will display the Regular Season rankings. These include only regular season game rankings. No playoffs.

Post Season: This will display only Post Season game rankings.

Total Season: This will display the Regular and Post Season rankings. This is the number that will determine weather you become King or Queen of the Daredevil ducks season!

The Member Groups defaults to the no group selected. You can choose any of the groups. Once selected, the rankings will be reordered into group member wins order. If you wish to start a group of your own please feel free to contact us!

The Hide Experts or Show Experts will either hide or show professional football organizations or individuals that we track to give folks a sense of how they compare. You will find retired football coaches, as well as, professional betting resources.

Understanding the Ranking List

Displayed are the members wins rankings for the selected Season and Game Type. This example shows four lines from the list.

The first column is Rank. It displays the ranking of each member based on wins in descending order of win totals.

The second column is Leader. It displays the member icon and screen name of each member. Clicking on the members icon will display a pop up with that members information.

The third column is W L T. It displays the members W = Wins, L = Losses and T = ties for the selected Season and Game Type.

Survey Results Help

Summary

The Survey Results page is where a member can review some of the various statistics regarding our recent survey.

You can see who has done what by clicking on any of the numbers in the top section.

You can review all the Answer Comments members made on any survey number by clicking on the survey question.

Details
Viewing and Understanding the top section

This section displays numbers for discrete statistics. Example: Surveys Started. Clicking on any of these numbers will display a list of members that satisfied that statistic.

The   Yes   and   No   items represent the Yes and No colors for the progress bars displayed on the survey Answer Results in the list below.

Understanding the Survey Question List

The survey question answer list displays three columns: Nbr, Question and Answer Results.

The first column Nbr displays the survey question number.

The second column Question displays question text for the survey question number. If you click on the survey question text, a pop up will be displayed of all the member Answer Comments for that survey particular question.

The third column Answer Results displays two bars in a graph showing the   Yes   and   No   answers for the survey question.

Weekly Total Leaders Help

Summary

The Weekly Total Leaders page is where a member can view member rankings for the week selected. This is a great way to get you bragging fix to a weekly basis!

You can select the Season, Week and Member Groups. You can also Show or Hide the addition of Experts to the ranking list.

The Experts are football processionals that pick games during the season. We collect their picks and add them so that you can see how you and others compare.

The ranking list displays all members in games won ranking order

Details
Selecting and viewing the results

This is where you can select Season, Week, Member Groups and Experts.

The Season defaults to the current season. Changing the season will bring up rankings associated with that year.

The Week defaults to the current week. You can choose any week in the current season. You can also select a previous season to see historical rankings.

The Member Groups defaults to the no group selected. You can choose any of the groups. Once selected, the rankings will be reordered into group member weekly wins order. If you wish to start a group of your own please feel free to contact us!

The Hide Experts or Show Experts will either hide or show professional football organizations or individuals that we track to give folks a sense of how they compare. You will find retired football coaches, as well as, professional betting resources.

Understanding the Ranking List

Displayed are the members weekly wins rankings for the selected Season and Week. This example shows two lines from the list.

The first column is Rank. It displays the ranking of each member based on week wins in descending order of week win totals.

The second column is Dates. It displays the dates which comprise the selected week.

The third column is Player. It displays the member icon and screen name of each member. Clicking on the members icon will display a pop up with that members information.

We have changed Team Weekly Ranking (under Teams) to Team Scores and Stats. We did this because the page shows both team stats and all games the selected team played and how they fared. It's a great way to see how a team has done in head to head against all comers for the selected season!

We have updated our Total Wins Leaders (under Member Stats). We changed the label for Percent to Mbrs Wins / All Games Played and added a new column Mbrs Wins / Mbrs Picks to show both wins against all games played in the season and wins against member picks made. Remember that winning the coveted DareDevil Ducks trophy is determined by total wins in a season. So percentage remains only a bragging right!

We have also changed the showing of Expert picks for both Total Wins Leaders and Weekly Totals Leaders (under Member Stats). Showing the Expert picks must now be chosen by selecting the Show Experts button!

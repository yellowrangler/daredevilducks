Did you know and Updates!

Our NFL Headlines web page has changed! You can now restrict what is returned from the selected News Source! Simply type in the item you are looking for in the Search input field and then click on the Get News button.

The NFL Headlines web page is located under the Teams menu.


We have added Injury Reports selection under the Teams menu. On the desktop web pages, selecting Injury Reports will display a choice of NFL Injury Report, Covers Injury Report and Yahoo Injury Report. Each displays player injuries for the week (which is selectable). All three have strengths. Check them out!

All this new functionality is available in both our Desktop and Mobile websites. Enjoy!

We have added a screen and renamed another. Under the button Games the second item is now called Each Player's picks for week. It is the same functionality as before (you can use this selection to see the picks of any player by selecting the player, season and week).

The third item under the button Games is now Players Picks for Week by Team. This selection lists a week's games with the number of player picks for each team displayed. This is a great way to see what teams are getting the most picks for a week!

Example: Go to Games and select Players Picks for Week by Team. Select Season: 2015 and Week: 2. Look at the first Game Kansas City Chiefs vs Denver Broncos. From the Picks column you can see that 6 players picked Kansas and 15 players picked Denver!

You can change the Season and Week by selecting from the provided dropdowns to view different combinations of total picks for seasons and weeks.

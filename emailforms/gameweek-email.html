We have made more changes to the Daredevil Ducks Website. They are as follows:

  1. You can now update your own personal information by clicking on your Avatar at the top right of the website. This will allow you to:
    • Change all personal information but your avatar. You still need to do that through us. But we are working on that.
    • You can now select your Favorite Team. This selection currently will be used when you go to the Team Weekly Ranking screen. It will preselect YOUR Favorite team from the list of teams.
    • You can add a little about yourself in the new field Biography.
    • There is also a check box for Please no eMail's. It is for future use.
  2. We made a change to the Team Weekly Ranking screen. There is now a sort button under the Week column heading. Click on this, and it will toggle sorting the information.
  3. All NFL Team web sites are now accessible in Team Discovery. You can still access any team's website by clicking on their NFL logo.
  4. The Hall of Fame screen now shows a vacant spot for the Dare Devil Ducks 2015 winner! Remember this could be YOU!
  5. We moved all the selection lists down a line to make these screens more useable on smaller devices.
  6. All the NFL Icons were re-mastered to be more scaleable and cleaner.
  7. We continue to make overall improvements for a better user experience on smaller devices. Still lots of work to be done here as this is designed to be primarily a desktop application.
